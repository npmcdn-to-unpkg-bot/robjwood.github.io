Griffith Jones

1888

Born on 26 June in Llwyn Penddu, Llanllechid, Caernarvon, Wales. His parents are Henry Jones (Quarry Labourer who was living in Llwyn Penddu, Llanllechid) and Ann Jones (nee Griffith).

1891

Lived in Llwyn Penddu cottage (family lived there from 1850's to 1947)

1909

  • Married Margaret Hughes on 23 April in The Parish Church, Llanllechid.
  • He was a Quarryman at the time.
  • He was living at Llwyn Penddu.
  • Witnesses were:
    • William H(?) Jones
    • Mary Hughes

1910

Had Annie Jones.

1911

  • Lived at 3 Glanrafon, Bont Uchaf, Bethesda.
  • Was a 'labourer in a slate quarry'

1916 (ish)

  • Fought in WW1
  • Possible (probably not) service numbers: 12442, 25483
  • Shot at, or near, Mametz Wood (we think Nain (Jennie Jones) has part of the bullet that he was shot with).
  • Discharged and treated at Bangor C & E hospital?

1944

On 26 March 1944 he and Margaret were living at 2 Fron Bant, Gerlan, Bethesda, Nr. Bangor, N. Wales.
