Ivor Jones

1926

Born in Bangor on 4 October.

1944

  • Taid was in the Royal Welsh Fusiliers, and joined in 1944.
  • What battalion was he in? 4th, 6th, 7th?
  • He spent much of his active time in Germany, and was in Berlin for a while.
  • He was involved in helping at Belsen concentration camp, and saw some horrible things.
  • I think he was briefly a corporal/lieutenant (Really? He would have been 18 in 1944).
  • Part of his training was in Brecon (Royal Welch Regiment), and some in Bradford (West Yorkshire Regiment).
  • He first travelled to the continent, from Harwich to the Hook of Holland (I think).

1950

Married Jenny Rees Tyrer 26 December.

1961

Lived at 15 Sandford Road, Moseley.

1970

Lived at 52 Runnymede Road, Birmingham.

2005

He died on 10 December.

Bethesda County School report

We found this report at Nain's:

Ivor Jones has attended this school regularly and punctually for two sessions and a term and during this period he has not progressed much in academic subjects, but has been interested in practical subjects.

He is a quiet lad whose conduct has been most satisfactory and in our experience he is truthful, obediant and reliable. While not likely to succeed in studious pursuits I have no doubt he will succeed in some form of craftwork.
