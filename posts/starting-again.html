Starting again

Depending on when you're reading this, it may look like this website's broken. By broken, I mean readable, fast and easy to use.

The only styles are a maximum-width here, and font-weight there.

Why?

I want to start from basically nothing and only add a little at a time.

I also want to start documenting what I'm doing a little more and worrying less about writing a prefectly worded post.

I've found that writing helps me think through a problem better and putting it out in public will hopefully persuade me to focus a little more.

Why else?

Over the last year or so I've started to notice some of the older website out and wondered why.

I was partly inspired my Micah Godbolt's website which has no styles at all. I also remember seeing Richard Stallman's website and being struck by how it looked. Mainly because I thought I'd time-travelled to 1995. I liked that.

The reason I've done it is to try and focus on the content first and actually get some changes live withouth my usual excuses. Excuses like:

Once I've sorted the way the font-size changes on a tablet device I'll launch it.

Or:

I can't launch anything without adjusting the order of the comments in the CSS.

Nobody cares.

I haven't even bothered with Google Analytics this time either as it didn't reallt help me. I spent more time filtering out spam so I could more easily view the behaviour of my six visitors a month.

Anyway, I'll probably change my mind but for now, here it is.
