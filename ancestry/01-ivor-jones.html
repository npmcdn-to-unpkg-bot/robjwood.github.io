Taid

  • Taid was in the Royal Welsh Fusiliers, and joined in 1944.
  • He spent much of his active time in Germany, and was in Berlin for a while.
  • He was involved in helping at Belsen concentration camp, and saw some horrible things.
  • I think he was briefly a corporal/lieutenant.
  • Part of his training was in Brecon (Royal Welch Regiment), and some in Bradford (West Yorkshire Regiment).
  • He first travelled to the continent, from Harwich to the Hook of Holland (I think).

1961

Lived at 15 Sandford Road, Moseley.
