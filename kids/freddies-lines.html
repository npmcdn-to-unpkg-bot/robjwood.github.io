Freddie's lines

Reasons why my son is already turning into an old man:

  • He wanted a memory foam pillow for his birthday.
  • His favourite food is a cheese ploughman's sandwich.
  • He wanted a daffodil because 'nobody's ever bough me flowers'

No. Oh, that's good. Now it's my head.

Nobody's ever bought me flowers.
